Privacy Policy

Application: Fishwich
Developer: Fishwich Studios
Last updated: 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fishwich Studios ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the Fishwich mobile application ("the App") available on Google Play. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Please read this policy carefully. By installing or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

Fishwich is developed and published by Fishwich Studios, owned and operated by Andrew Linstead, based in Santa Pola, Alicante, Spain. For data protection purposes, Fishwich Studios is the data controller.

2. What the App Does

Fishwich is a caller announcement application. When your phone receives an incoming call, the App reads the caller's contact name from your device, matches it against local keyword categories, and plays a spoken announcement. The App is available in English, French, German, and Castilian Spanish.

3. Data We Access

Contact names

The App reads contact names stored on your device for the sole purpose of identifying the caller category and generating the appropriate spoken announcement. Contact names are processed entirely on your device. They are read locally at the time of an incoming call, used to generate the announcement, and stored locally in the App's on-device call history for announced calls. No contact names or any other contact information are transmitted off your device to us or to any third party.

Call history

The App maintains a local call history on your device for calls that have been announced. This data is stored solely on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. You can clear this history at any time within the App.

In-app purchase information

Purchases made within the App are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, receive, or store any payment card details or financial information. Google's own privacy policy governs the processing of payment data. You can view Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

Fishwich does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or phone number
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Microphone recordings or voice data
  • Any data from your contacts beyond the local, on-device name matching described in Section 3

5. Third Party Services and SDKs

The App does not integrate any third party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs. No data is shared with third party services by the App itself.

Google Play Services operates independently on your device and may collect certain device-level data in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. We have no control over and no access to any data collected by Google Play Services.

6. Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

  • READ_CONTACTS — required to read the incoming caller's contact name for announcement purposes. This is core to the App's primary function and cannot be achieved without this permission. Access is temporary, occurring only at the moment of an incoming call.
  • READ_CALL_LOG / PHONE_STATE — required to detect incoming calls and trigger the announcement.

You may revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings. If you revoke the READ_CONTACTS permission, the App will not be able to match caller names and will announce calls using a generic announcement.

7. Data Storage and Security

All data processed by the App remains on your device at all times. We do not operate servers that receive or store personal data from the App. Because no data is transmitted off your device, risks associated with data interception or server-side data breaches do not apply to Fishwich.

On-device data, including local call history, is stored using Android's standard application storage mechanisms and is accessible only to the App.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Contact names are accessed transiently at the time of an incoming call and retained only within the on-device call history. You can clear the call history at any time from within the App. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored App data from your device.

Because Fishwich does not collect or transmit personal data, the majority of GDPR data subject rights do not apply in practice. Nevertheless, you retain the following rights where applicable:

  • Right of access — the right to know what personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — the right to have inaccurate personal data corrected
  • Right to erasure — the right to request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restriction of processing — the right to request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — the right to receive your personal data in a structured format
  • Right to object — the right to object to our processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have any privacy-related questions or complaints, please contact us using the details in Section 11 below. We will respond within one month as required by GDPR.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the AEPD at www.aepd.es.
